Dogwood Garden Club


We Strive to grow and beautify

 

The Dogwood Garden Club held its first meeting in September, 1959. The hostess for for this meeting was Mrs. I.M. Planting who opened her home at 99 Green Drive to the founding members.
Dogwood Garden Club has been active in the community since its founding and remains so to this present date.
